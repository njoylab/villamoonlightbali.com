Private pool and garden

Nestled just minutes away from the picturesque Berawa Beach, our villa boasts a unique private beach area and a lush garden, creating a tranquil oasis for your stay. The highlight is our stunning pool, offering breathtaking views – a perfect spot to unwind and soak up the Balinese sun.

Discover Canggu’s Beaches and Local Charm

Explore the local charm with a leisurely 12-minute walk to Nelayan Beach or venture a bit further to discover the vibrant Canggu Beach, only 1.3 miles away.
For your convenience, our friendly staff can arrange shuttle services, and with the Ngurah Rai International Airport just 9.3 miles away, your travel will be hassle-free.
