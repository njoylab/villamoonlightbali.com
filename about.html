Welcome to Your Bali Oasis

A 3-Bedroom Villa with Private Pool

Villa Overview

Escape to our exquisite villa nestled in a prestigious area of Bali. This 3-bedroom sanctuary is a blend of comfort and luxury, featuring:

  • A private pool amidst tropical greenery
  • An airy open living area with pool views
  • Each bedroom boasts an ensuite bathroom
  • On-premises parking for added convenience
  • High-speed Wi-Fi connection
  • 24/7 on-demand guest services

The Living Space

Relax in our enclosed living area, complete with a cozy sofa, coffee table, smart TV, air conditioning, and direct pool view.

Gourmet Kitchen

The kitchen is equipped with all you need: stove, microwave, fridge, freezer, blender, toaster, kettle, water dispenser, and full cutlery set. Adjoining the kitchen is a dining island for intimate meals.

Restful Bedrooms

Each bedroom is designed for comfort and relaxation, featuring classic furnishings and bedside tables.

Luxurious Bathrooms

Experience the soothing ambiance in our bathrooms, each with a shower, bathtub, and all essential amenities.

Dining Area

The dining room, set between the living area and kitchenette, features a teak wood table and matching chairs, adding an ethnic touch to your dining experience.

Private Pool

Dive into the refreshing waters of your private pool, flanked by lush tropical greenery for the ultimate relaxation.

Guest Access & Services

Our villa is a haven of tranquility, offering cozy living spaces and a well-equipped kitchen. It's perfect for making lasting memories. Upon booking, our dedicated Hombali hospitality team is at your service for any inquiries.

Additional Information:

  • Airport pick-up available upon request
  • Check-in: 2 pm | Check-out: 12 pm. Early or late services subject to availability and may incur additional charges
  • Daily housekeeping provided. Linen and towel changes every three days or upon request
  • Please note: No pets allowed
